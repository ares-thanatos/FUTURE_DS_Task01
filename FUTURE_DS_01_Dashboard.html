Actionable Insights & Recommendations
📈

Electronics Dominates Revenue

Electronics accounts for the largest revenue share, led by Headphones (₹4.61M) and Smart Watch (₹4.40M). Invest more in Electronics marketing and stock depth — especially in East region.

⚠️

South Region is Underperforming

The South region consistently lags behind other regions. Launch targeted regional campaigns, offer region-specific promotions, and strengthen distributor partnerships in the South.

🌟

May is the Peak Revenue Month

May 2024 recorded the highest revenue (₹3.67M). Plan major product launches and promotional events around April–May to capitalize on seasonal demand spikes.

💸

Discount Strategy Needs Review

11.5% of total revenue comes from orders with discounts above 25%. This erodes profit margins. Consider tiered discount caps and loyalty programs instead of blanket discounts.
