Actionable Insights & Recommendations
💸
Price is the #1 churn driver (32.3%)
122 customers left citing high price. Introduce annual plans with 20–30% discount and a "pause" option to retain price-sensitive users before they cancel.
⏱️
First 6 months are the danger window
Churned customers leave at avg 6 months vs 14.6 for active. Launch automated onboarding sequences at day 1, 7, 14, and 30 with in-app feature discovery.
⬆️
Upsell Basic customers to reduce churn
Basic plan churns at 34% vs Premium at 27%. A targeted upgrade campaign boosts both retention and MRR simultaneously.
🔗
Fix referral channel — highest churn (34%)
Referral customers churn most despite low CAC. Revisit referral messaging and add a dedicated onboarding variant for referred signups.
