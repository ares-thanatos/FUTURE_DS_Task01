## 4. Revenue Trend Analysis

### 4.1 Monthly Revenue

Monthly revenue ranged from ₹2.27M (July) to ₹3.67M (May). Key observations:
- **Jan–May**: Generally strong with a peak in May
- **Jun–Jul**: Notable dip, possibly mid-year slowdown
- **Aug–Sep**: Recovery phase with Sep hitting ₹3.67M
- **Oct–Dec**: Gradual decline toward year end

**Year-over-year growth (Jan→Dec): -16.3%** — indicating the business needs to address seasonality and Q4 demand stimulation.

- Average profit margin across products: **~52%**

## 9. Actionable Recommendations

### 9.1 Double Down on Electronics
Electronics is the growth engine. Increase inventory, marketing spend, and cross-sell opportunities for Headphones and Smart Watches — the top two revenue drivers.

### 9.2 Revive South Region
The South region needs dedicated attention. Recommended actions:
- Regional brand awareness campaigns
- Exclusive South region promotional offers
- Strengthen last-mile delivery partnerships

### 9.3 Stimulate Q4 Demand
Q4 is the weakest quarter. Launch festival and year-end sales events (Oct–Dec) targeting Apparel and Home & Living categories to offset seasonal dip.

### 9.4 Reform Discount Strategy
Replace blanket discounts with:
- Tiered loyalty rewards for repeat customers
- Bundle discounts instead of flat % off
- Price anchoring for premium products like Laptops and Smartphones

### 9.5 Grow Sports Category
Sports is the smallest category (10.4% share) but has strong unit economics. Partner with fitness influencers and launch seasonal fitness campaigns to drive growth.

---

## 10. Conclusion

FY 2024 showed strong Electronics performance but revealed regional disparities and discount inefficiencies. With focused strategies on South region growth, Q4 demand generation, and smarter discounting, the business can target **+20–25% revenue growth in FY 2025**.
